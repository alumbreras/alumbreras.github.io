I am currently a PhD student in the ERIC Lab at Université Lyon 2 under the supervision of Bertrand Jouve and Julien Velcin. I am doing my thesis at Technicolor R&D. Previously, I got a MSc in Artificial Intelligence from Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya and wrote a Master’s Thesis on Trust-Aware Recommender Systems advised by Ricard Gavaldà.

Before that, I studied Telecommunication Engineering and worked for seven years at Telefónica I+D (a.k.a Telefonica Research) as a research engineer. I am also a member of the Datanalysis15m research group. (Universitat Oberta de Catalunya).

Some of my research interests are Machine Learning, Bayesian Statistics, Complex Systems and Social Network Analysis

Update: From February 2016 I'm at IRIT as invited PhD student.
